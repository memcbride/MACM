Execution Report:

After the close of each trading session, you can find out whether or not your orders were executed. Go to the main page and find the section entitled "Past Orders Placed." The orders from the previous trading session will be listed. The last column will contain the number of shares from the order that were executed.

In this example, all 100 shares were executed. One hundred RED shares were purchased at a market price of $20 per share for a total of $2000. Your cash balance is therefore $2000 after the transaction.

The market price is determined by supply and demand. Detail on the determination of market prices is contained in the market clearing report.
