Editing Your Account

After you log on to the website, you may wish to change your password. Press the "Edit User Info" Link. A screen will appear which contains the following form:

Name Password EMail Get EMail
Rob

 

To edit the user information, enter new information in the fields provided.

  • The Password should contain no blank spaces and can contain characters and numbers but must start with a character.
  • The EMail must be a valid e-mail address.
  • The GetEMail field should be yes or no.

Enter your new password in the appropriate field, and press "Update." You are also encouraged to enter an email address, where we can contact you if necessary. Enter the address in the field, and enter "yes" in the "Get EMail" field.
