Status:

The status section is the main entry point into the CAPM experiment. The status page provides three main pieces of information.

Market Status:

In the upper left side the page welcomes the user, indicates whether the market is open or closed, and indicates the current Round. For example -

Welcome: Amos
Market: Open
Round: 1

In this example, Amos is logged in. The current round is the first, but the market is open.

Current Asset Status:

The "Current Asset Status" section indicates the current number of shares and cash balances, as well as past balances.

Current Asset Status

Asset Balances
Round Cash Red White Blue Green Gold
initial 4000.0 0 0 0 200 75
1 2000.0 100 0 0 200 75

In this example, 100 shares of Red were purchased at a market price of $20 in the 1st round. The Red shares were increased by 100 and the cash balances were decreased by $2000.

The market price is determined by supply and demand. Details on the determination of market prices is contained in the market clearing report.

Current Orders Placed:

The "Current Orders Placed" section lists the orders for the current round. For each order, the OrderID, Round, Type, Asset, Price, Quantity, and Date Placed are listed. For example -
