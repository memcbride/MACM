Instructions:

We have created a market for five types of risky assets identified by color: RED, WHITE, BLUE, GREEN and GOLD. During the experiment you will have several opportunities to purchase or sell each colored asset. At the end of the market, each asset will be liquidated for cash. The total amount of cash you obtain will be proportional to the number of grade points you earn.

If you follow the instructions carefully and make good decisions, you may earn a substantial number of grade points and raise your overall course grade.

However, be careful. Bad decisions and/or bad luck may lower your grade in this class.
