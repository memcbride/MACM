Asset Valuation:

The liquidation values for the assets will be determined using a random drawing. A bingo cage will contain eight balls numbered 1 through 8. Two of the balls will be chosen (without replacement), and these numbers will be announced after the close of the last trading session.

Each asset will then be liquidated according to the following formulas:

RED
(The first number chose squared) - 1
WHITE
10(The absolute difference between the numbers drawn)-10
BLUE
15(The maximum of the numbers drawn - The second number drawn)
GREEN
(8-The maximum of the numbers drawn)*(8+The minimum number drawn)
GOLD
30 for each number drawn that is less than 4

For example, suppose an "8" is the first number and a "3" is the second number chosen. According the the formulas, each RED asset will be worth 63, each WHITE will be worth 40, each BLUE will be worth 75, each GREEN will be worth 0 and each GOLD will be worth 30.
