Setting Up Your Account

To proceed, you will need a user ID and password. If you do not possess these, contact your instructor to obtain them.

To log in, open the specified web address. You may need to set your browser to accept cookies. A login screen will appear and will contain the login form shown below:

User ID:
Password:
As: Student Administrator

Enter your User ID and your password. Click "Student" and then press the "Login" button. The main screen will now appear.

The main screen is where you will have summary information about your current asset status, be able to track market activities by round, and most importantly place you limit orders.

The current asset status shows the initial share and cash balances. You will receive a mixture of shares and cash. The assets you receive may all be one color, or they may be a mixture of the five types. Other students may or may not start the exercise with the same mixture of assets and cash.
