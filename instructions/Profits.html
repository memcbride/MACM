Calculating Profits:

At the end of the experiment, the numbers drawn from the bingo cage will be revealed and the values of all five assets will be announced. Your net worth will equal the liquidation value of all your assets plus your cash on hand, less any funds you borrowed and any short sale penalties.

For example, suppose RED is worth 10, WHITE is worth 51, BLUE is worth 60, GREEN is worth 0 and GOLD is worth 30. You hold 10 shares of RED, 50 shares of WHITE, 20 shares of BLUE, no shares of GREEN, 100 shares of GOLD and $8400 in cash.
