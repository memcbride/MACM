Exectuion Report:

After the close of each trading session, you can find out whether or not your orders were exectued. From the menu options, find the section entitled "Past Orders Placed." You will be presented with the following:

Select the round for which you want the past orders and then click on Get Info?

Select a round from the menu and click on the "Get Info" button. The orders from the previous trading sessions will be available. Select a Round and click on "GetInfo."

The orders from the requested Round will be listed. The last column will contain the number of shares from the order that were executed.
