Trading Schedule:

There will be four trading sessions.

You may place limit orders during any sessions. Orders placed in one session will not be carried over to the next session. You may place additional orders at any time provided the market is open. ONCE SUBMITTED, ORDERS CANNOT BE CANCELLED.

Session 1

Monday, Nov. 13th - Friday, Nov. 17th

Session 2

Saturday, Nov. 18th - Wed, Nov. 22nd

Session 3

Thurs, Nov. 23rd - Saturday, Nov. 25th

Session 4

Sunday, Nov. 26th - Wed, Nov. 29th

DRAWING

at the Final Exam
